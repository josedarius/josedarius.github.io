Enterprise Kubernetes Platform

A secure, scalable, and governed Kubernetes platform that enables product teams to self-serve — without compromising security, reliability, or cost control.

The challenge

Kubernetes adoption often starts fast — but quickly becomes fragmented. Teams create clusters with inconsistent security, no clear ownership, limited observability, and uncontrolled costs.

Without a standardized platform, organizations struggle with cluster sprawl, operational risk, compliance gaps, and developer friction.

Our solution

MyCloudSME designs and delivers enterprise Kubernetes platforms on AKS, EKS, and GKE — built with security, governance, and operability embedded from day one.

Platform-first design

Standardized clusters, namespaces, and environments designed for multi-team usage.

Security by default

Identity integration, network policies, secrets management, and workload isolation built in.

Developer self-service

Golden paths, templates, and CI/CD integrations that accelerate delivery safely.

Operational excellence

Observability, autoscaling, backup, DR, and SRE practices included.
