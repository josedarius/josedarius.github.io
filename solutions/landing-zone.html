Landing Zone & Governance at Scale

Standardized, secure, and scalable cloud foundations that enable multiple teams to operate independently — without losing control, security, or visibility.

The challenge

Many organizations adopt cloud quickly — but without a standardized foundation. The result is inconsistent networking, fragmented identity models, unmanaged security policies, uncontrolled costs, and operational chaos as teams scale.

A well-designed landing zone solves this by providing a governed, repeatable, and secure foundation that enables speed without sacrificing control.

Our solution

MyCloudSME designs and implements enterprise-grade landing zones aligned to Azure CAF, AWS CAF, and GCP best practices — customized to your operating model, compliance requirements, and scale.

Standardized architecture

Consistent subscription, account, and project structures with clear ownership boundaries.

Secure identity & access

Centralized identity, least-privilege access, and role-based controls embedded by default.

Network & connectivity

Hub-and-spoke or mesh designs with controlled ingress, egress, and hybrid connectivity.

Governance & policy

Policy-as-code enforcing security, compliance, tagging, and cost controls automatically.
