Data Center Exit & Cloud Adoption

A structured, low-risk approach to migrate workloads from on-premise environments to cloud — without business disruption.

The challenge

Many organizations face rising data center costs, aging infrastructure, expiring contracts, and limited scalability.

A data center exit is not just a technical migration — it requires careful planning, risk management, application classification, and operational readiness.

Our approach

We deliver data center exits using a **factory-based migration model** that balances speed, stability, and modernization.

  • Application and dependency discovery
  • 6R classification (Rehost, Replatform, Refactor, etc.)
  • Landing zone and governance setup
  • Wave-based migration execution
  • Post-migration stabilization and optimization

How we deliver

01

Discover & Assess

Inventory applications, dependencies, data flows, and business criticality.

02

Classify & Plan

Apply the 6R model and define migration waves, priorities, and risk mitigation.

03

Prepare Cloud Foundations

Build landing zones, networking, identity, security, and governance.

04

Migrate in Waves

Execute migrations with rollback plans, validation checkpoints, and stakeholder communication.

05

Stabilize & Optimize

Post-migration tuning, cost optimization, security hardening, and operations handover.

Outcomes

  • Reduced infrastructure and data center costs
  • Improved scalability and resilience
  • Standardized security and governance
  • Clear operational ownership in cloud
