TagOps

AI-Intelligent Tagging & Compliance Engine

TagOps is an AI-driven tagging and compliance engine that brings structure, ownership, and governance readiness to complex Azure environments.

What TagOps Solves

  • Inconsistent or missing tags across subscriptions
  • Lack of ownership visibility for cloud resources
  • Audit and compliance readiness gaps
  • Unreliable cost allocation caused by weak tagging

Core Capabilities

Mandatory Tag Enforcement
Dynamically enforces mandatory tags based on resource type, environment, and workload context.

AI-Based Tag Recommendations
Uses naming patterns, metadata, and usage signals to recommend corrective tags at scale.

Tagging Quality & Readiness Score
Measures tagging maturity for governance, security, and FinOps use cases.

TagOps forms the intelligent tagging foundation for MyOps.
