Cloud Migration & Modernization

Structured, risk-controlled migration and modernization programs — designed to move workloads safely, modernize where it matters, and avoid costly rework after go-live.

What this service is

Cloud migration is not just about moving servers. It is a transformation of operating models, security controls, cost structures, and delivery workflows.

MyCloudSME delivers migration programs that combine technical execution, security-by-design, and business continuity. We focus on predictable outcomes — not rushed lift-and-shift projects that create technical debt in the cloud.

Why organizations need migration & modernization

  • Data center exits or contract expirations
  • Rising infrastructure and licensing costs
  • Security, compliance, or audit pressure
  • Legacy platforms blocking scalability and agility
  • Inconsistent environments across regions or teams
  • Leadership demands for measurable ROI from cloud investments

Without a structured approach, migrations often lead to cost overruns, unstable platforms, and operational complexity. Our model is designed to prevent exactly that.

How we deliver

A structured, risk-controlled delivery approach designed for enterprise migrations and modernization programs.

01

Discover

Assess business drivers, timelines, compliance constraints, application criticality, and current infrastructure dependencies.

02

Assess & Classify

Application and workload assessment using the 6R model (Rehost, Replatform, Refactor, Retire, Retain, Replace), including cost, risk, and modernization potential.

03

Design Target State

Define landing zones, network topology, identity model, security baselines, and migration wave strategy.

04

Migrate in Waves

Execute migrations in controlled waves with rollback plans, validation checkpoints, and production readiness gates.

05

Modernize Selectively

Apply modernization where it delivers value — platform services, automation, scalability, and resilience improvements.

06

Stabilize & Optimize

Post-migration stabilization, FinOps optimization, security hardening, and operational handover.
