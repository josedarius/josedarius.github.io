Kubernetes & Container Platforms

Design, build, and operate secure Kubernetes platforms that enable product teams to deploy faster while maintaining governance, reliability, and cost control.

What this service is

Our Kubernetes & Container Platforms service helps organizations adopt and scale containerized workloads using enterprise-ready Kubernetes platforms such as AKS, EKS, and GKE.

We focus on building **platforms, not just clusters** — integrating identity, networking, security, CI/CD, observability, and operational controls so Kubernetes becomes a reliable foundation for product teams, not a source of risk.

When this service makes sense

  • Multiple teams deploying containerized applications
  • Inconsistent or insecure Kubernetes cluster setups
  • Lack of governance, visibility, or cost control
  • Operational incidents caused by misconfigured clusters
  • Need for standardized CI/CD and self-service deployment

How we deliver

A secure, production-ready Kubernetes delivery model aligned to enterprise operating standards.

Platform Strategy & Design

02

03

Observability & Reliability

06

Cost optimization, capacity planning, upgrades, security patching, and operational handover.
