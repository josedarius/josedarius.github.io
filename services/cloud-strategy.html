Cloud Strategy & Advisory

A pragmatic, execution-ready cloud strategy that aligns technology, security, cost, and operating models — not just high-level diagrams.

What this service is

Cloud Strategy & Advisory helps organizations define a clear, actionable path to cloud adoption or optimization. We work with leadership, architects, and engineering teams to align business goals with technical reality — ensuring the strategy can actually be delivered.

Unlike traditional consulting approaches that stop at slides, our strategies are designed to translate directly into landing zones, security baselines, migration waves, and delivery backlogs.

When you need Cloud Strategy & Advisory

  • No clear cloud direction or inconsistent adoption across teams
  • Upcoming data center exit or major platform refresh
  • Cloud costs increasing without visibility or control
  • Security, compliance, or audit concerns in cloud environments
  • Multiple teams deploying differently with no governance
  • Leadership needs clear ROI, roadmap, and risk visibility
